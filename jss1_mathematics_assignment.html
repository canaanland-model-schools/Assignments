Mathematics Assignment

Class: Junior Secondary
Due Date: April 15, 2026
Total Marks: 50

Assignment Instructions

Complete all questions below. Show all your working clearly.

Use foolscap sheets or an A4 exercise book. Write your name, class, and number on the front page.

Questions

Question 1 (10 marks): Simplify the following algebraic expressions:

a) 3x + 5y - 2x + 3y

b) 2(3a - 4b) + 5(a + 2b)

c) (x² + 3x - 4) + (2x² - 5x + 6)

Question 2 (15 marks): Solve the following equations:

a) 2x + 7 = 15

b) 3(y - 4) = 2y + 5

c) (2z/3) - 5 = 1

Question 3 (10 marks): Geometry

Calculate the perimeter and area of a rectangle with length 12cm and width 8cm.

Calculate the area of a triangle with base 10cm and height 6cm.

Question 4 (15 marks): Word Problems

a) If 5 books cost ₦1,250, how much would 8 books cost?

b) A father is three times as old as his son. If the sum of their ages is 60 years, find their ages.

c) Share ₦4,500 in the ratio 2:3:4 between three people.

Marking Scheme

1 mark will be awarded for each correct step in the working. Final answers without working will receive half marks only.
